Crystals Collector

This is a simple hero unit, a simple jumbotron-style component for calling extra attention to featured content or information.

Instructions on how to play the game:

  1. Click on the play button to start the game:

  2. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  3. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score

  4. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

  5. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

  7. The value of each crystal will change upon every new round.
